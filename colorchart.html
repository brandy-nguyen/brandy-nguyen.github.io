Gold is the most fascinating color out of my other favorite colors. The lustrous shine of the rich yellow color is so appealing that nearly all cultures perceived it to be a positive color. It symbolizes prosperity, wealth, and divinity as a common theme among cultures infatuated with the color. In fact, I've seen this in Asian culture in things like the Lunar New Year festivals. The colors of red and gold together are popular in Asian cultures since they represent prosperit and wealth respectively. Over all, gold is a visually pleasing color that nearly everyone accepts as a positive color.
