Black is often seen as an ominous color that represents death and impurity. It invokes feelings of sadness. As black is the absence of color, it symbolizes lack of light and the darkness of the human spirit. Black is a neutral color since it lacks color therefore allowing other colors to be noticeable without it being the main focus.

Although black is seen in a negative light, it does have positive connotations. In modern fashion, the color is used to depict eliteness, professionalism, and confidence. A sense of one's authority can be derived from their choice in using black whether for business or popularity.
